Wa-Kushma is an independently run Mexican Folk Dance company located in Chula Vista, California , whose mission is to show the culture of Mexico through dance.

About Us

Our mission is to share the traditional dances and culture of Mexico with Southern California. We envision giving students of all ages and backgrounds the opportunity to learn and experience the traditions of Mexico through music, dances, and traveling.

Since its foundation in 2005, Wa-Kushma has grown in various aspects, earning reputable prestige all thorughout Mexico and parts of the US.
